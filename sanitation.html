CP FITTINGS

Made from high-quality brass or metal with a durable chrome finish, CP fittings add a sleek and polished look to bathrooms and kitchens. They are resistant to corrosion and long-lasting, making them ideal for taps, showers, and other plumbing fixtures.

PTMT FITTINGS

Lightweight yet strong, PTMT fittings are made from high-grade thermoplastic, offering excellent resistance to rust, corrosion, and chemicals. They provide a cost-effective and durable alternative to metal fittings, commonly used in faucets, taps, and other sanitary applications.
